About Me

Hello and welcome to My Personal Portfolio Website. First of all, my name is Alex, I am originally from Romania and I live in London, United Kingdom. My passion for technology started when I was very young. I have been using a computer since I was 5 years old and since then, everything has changed. I was learning and discovering new things about technology and this fascinated me a lot.


I started developing a real passion and curiosity for websites. I like the way a website is designed, and I wanted to learn more about how this all works. First of all, I have tried experimenting with Easy Website Builders, such as Wix, Shopify etc. Then I have moved on to Wordpress which was way more complex and challenging. Now, here I am building websites from scratch, using pure code and nothing else.
